## Quick Start

1. Open `index.html` in your browser
2. Start adding tasks in the input field
3. Click "Add Task" or press Enter
4. Use checkboxes to mark tasks as complete
5. Click "Edit" to modify a task
6. Click "Delete" to remove a task
7. Use filters to organize your view

Your tasks are automatically saved and will persist even after closing the browser.
